Frequently Asked Questions

What is QBoosting Store?

QBoosting Store provides professional in-game boosting services to help players reach higher ranks and unlock rewards. We do not sell accounts, in-game currency, or items.

Is boosting safe for my account?

Yes, we only work with experienced professional players who play manually without using hacks, cheats, or third-party software. However, boosting always carries some risk under the game publisher's rules, so you use our service at your own discretion.

What information do I need to provide?

We only require your Sony login details (email and password) and any verification codes sent by Sony. We never ask for your WebApp details or other personal information.

Can I log in to my account during the boosting process?

No. While the booster is working on your account, you must not log in. If you log in during this period, it may interrupt the process, and we are not responsible for any negative effects.

How long does the boosting take?

Delivery time depends on the service you order and the game conditions. We will provide you with an estimated completion time before starting.

Do you use any hacks or cheats?

No. All boosting is performed manually by professional players to ensure your account remains safe.

What happens if you don't achieve my requested rank?

We cannot guarantee an exact rank, but we will give you a minimum guaranteed rank before starting. If we fail to achieve that minimum, you will receive a full refund.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are only possible if the service has not yet started. Once we begin, the payment becomes non-refundable unless we fail to deliver the agreed minimum rank.

How do I know when my order is completed?

Once your booster finishes, you will receive a notification through our system. We also recommend changing your account password immediately after completion.

What if I have a problem with my booster?

If your booster is unresponsive or you encounter any issue, contact our support immediately. If a booster contacts you directly outside our system (e.g., via Sony Messages) and you have proof, you'll receive a 50% discount on your next order.
